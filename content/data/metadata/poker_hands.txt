High Card
When no other hand is possible, scores whichever played card is the highest rank.

Pair
Two cards with a matching rank. Suits may be different.

Two Pair
Two cards with a matching rank and two cards with a different matching rank. Suits may be different.

Three of a Kind
Three cards with a matching rank. Suits may be different.

Straight
Five cards with ranks that are in consecutive order. Suits may be different. Aces can be counted as high (A K Q J 10) or low (A 2 3 4 5), but not at the same time (K A 2 3 4 is not valid).

Flush
Five cards of any rank that all share the same unit.

Full House
Three cards with a matching rank and two cards with a different matching rank, with cards from at least two or more suits.

Four of a Kind
Four cards with a matching rank. Suits may be different.

Straight Flush
Five cards in consecutive order that all share the same suit.

Five of a Kind
Secret
Five cards with the same rank which are not all the same unit.

Flush House
Secret
Three cards with the same rank, and two cards with the same rank, all from a single suit.

Flush Five
Secret
Five cards with the same rank and same unit.
